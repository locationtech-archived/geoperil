      Are you looking to learn more about what we are doing? Still trying to figure out how this might be applicable to your work? Looking to help?

      You’ve come to the right place.

      We’ll be building out resource around some of the pillars of scientific GPU computing, in particular the computation of models for simulating forecasts for geohazards and other natural hazards.

      We want you to join us. Here are a few ways you can get involved:
      • Get in contact and follow us: Martin (staff page, @mrtnhmtz, LinkedIn), Johannes, and Andrey (staff page).
      • Write an e-mail, ask questions and let us know what you’re doing.
      • Coming soon: Join our mailing list and stay up to date with our latest news as well as share your thoughts and questions.

      About

      TRIDEC Cloud is a platform and activity that will help researchers around the world use cloud based GPU computation services for analysing geohazards and other natural hazards. The website you use is an early alpha version for demonstration purposes to give the concept a whirl and to shape science’s future. Further functionality, improvements and possible profound changes will be implemented in the next weeks and months. If you would like to get involved then feel free to connect with us.

      However, the service is aimed for research purposes only. You may NOT use the service for early warning or crisis management purposes in its current version. So please note that:
